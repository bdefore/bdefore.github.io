Shelter From the Storm

1.

my pacemaker reminds me
we live in that new century
where the world is round, round, round
the past spills at my heels
and my future is a slippery slope
leading somewhere unseen

2.

now, you with your own tuggings
from a pandemonium pulse
from a heartbeat that whirls wherewithal
presenting: empress, priestess, star, lovers

you have spun me sideways
or frontways, slantways
and sometimes more original oddways:

one such muse thundered us into the thicket
never a shrug when we realized
that the newts could appreciate
the downpores more than our own

we took it all in
buckets of rain, veritas
i hope i haven't gotten my signals crossed
just because
you gave me shelter from the storm

soon i'll face not the hurricane but the typhoon
and i will spin counter-clockwise
in the mid of night i'll go wanderwondering
it'll be midnight whether it's 12 or not

i'll encounter the local perfume
and wonder where you are
remembering you, the one struck by lightning years ago
you've given me shelter from the storm
and might have gotten me struck in the process
